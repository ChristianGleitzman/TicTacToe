Player X Moves First

Tic Tac Toe is a two-player game. You can play against a friend or an AI by toggling the AI button. The goal is to place three of your symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Player X always goes first. The first player to get three in a row wins the game. If all of the spaces on the board are filled and no one has won, then the game is a stalemate.
